BlazeTap 🔥 is a reaction-based clicking game.

  • When the game starts, a grid of boxes appears.
  • You must click the highlighted red box before the timer runs out.
  • Each successful click increases your score and immediately starts the next round.
  • The time allowed decreases as your score gets higher, making the game harder.
  • If you fail to click the highlighted box in time, the game ends.
  • Your top score is shown and can be saved to a leaderboard.
